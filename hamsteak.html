Ham steak recipe

Here you see two deliciously ham steaks served well seasoned.

The ham steak is suited with a mango flavoured salad as a side dish, complemented by a hot sweet chili sauce

  • Ham
  • Mango
  • Salad
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Season the steak with a lot of salt and pepper.
  2. Sear the steak in the pan to give the ham a nice crust, keeping the juice within the ham while heating
  3. Put the ham in the oven for about 2 hours.
  4. Prepare the mango salad in the meantime
  5. Take out the ham when the degree has reached 70 celcius
